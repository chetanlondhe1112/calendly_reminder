Calendly Meeting Management

Features
- Display all the meeting events for the past 2 months and 2 months in future
- The meeting details are pulled using Calendly API
- The meeting details will contain the client name, meeting date, category of client, etc
- Another sheet of data will display the clients that have not had a meeting in the past2 months or in future
- The admin user should be able to select one or users from the list of clients and send a reminder message to the clients
- The reminder message is sent using a whatsapp template
- Another sheet of data will display the client name, meeting reminder sent date
- The whatsapp meeting template will be displayed in the UI and not editable
